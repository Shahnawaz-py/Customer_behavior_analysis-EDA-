## 📊 Customer Behavior Analysis (EDA Project)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on customer purchasing behavior data.

The goal is to understand customer patterns, product performance, and business insights to help improve decision making.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Libraries Used

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📂 Dataset Features

The dataset includes customer information such as:

- Customer ID
- Age, Gender
- Item Purchased, Category
- Purchase Amount (USD)
- Location, City
- Payment Method
- Shipping Type
- Review Rating
- Subscription Status
- Frequency of Purchases

---

## 📊 Key Business Insights

- Clothing category generates the highest revenue and also has the highest ratings.
- Male customers tend to purchase more than female customers.
- Customers prefer free shipping over paid shipping options.
- PayPal is the most preferred payment method, followed by credit card and cash.
- Non-subscribed customers generated more revenue compared to subscribed customers.
- Montana has the highest purchases.
- Footwear category has the highest average purchase value.
- Customer ID 2843 is the highest spending customer.

---

## 📈 Key Analysis Performed

- Data cleaning and missing value handling
- GroupBy analysis for categories, gender, and location
- Revenue and spending pattern analysis
- Customer segmentation
- Visualization using bar charts, pie charts, and scatter plots

---

## 🎯 Objective

To analyze customer behavior and extract meaningful business insights that can help improve marketing strategies, customer targeting, and revenue growth.
